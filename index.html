A path built at the meeting point
of engineering and advocacy.

Alia grew up in Giza with a scientist's curiosity and a builder's instinct for solving real problems. That instinct carried her to Istanbul, where she is completing a degree in Environmental Engineering at Istanbul University-Cerrahpaşa, and later to Budapest, where an Erasmus+ exchange in Water Science at Ludovika University of Public Service sharpened her technical grounding.

Today, her work spans environmental and social consulting for infrastructure and industrial projects, where she has contributed to ESIA, ESDD, EIA, RAP, proposal development, Public Consultation Workshops, and environmental and social management frameworks. Through these experiences, she has developed a strong foundation in sustainability consulting, stakeholder engagement, environmental compliance, and project planning.

Outside the office, she leads Green Gate 4 MENA Youth as Head of Programs & Impact, having previously scaled the organisation's operations as Chief Operating Officer to reach 500+ youth leaders across 22 countries. She was appointed one of Türkiye's 200 national Climate Envoys, represented Egypt as a Youth Delegate at COY19 in Baku, and now facilitates logistics for COY21 ahead of COP31 in Antalya. She works comfortably in Arabic, English, and Turkish, and believes the clearest engineering and the clearest advocacy are built from the same instinct: pay attention, then act.
