01 — Story

Once upon a time,
there was an adventure.

That's how Alia describes her own life — as a story still being written, chapter by chapter, continent by continent. Born in Egypt with a mind wired for science and a heart wired for impact, she moved to Istanbul and pursued a B.Eng in Environmental Engineering at Istanbul University-Cerrahpaşa. She didn't just cross a border — she crossed into a new way of thinking about engineering, sustainability, and the responsibility that comes with knowledge.

Now on an Erasmus+ exchange in Hungary, deepening her expertise in Water Science and Environmental Engineering at Ludovika University of Public Service, Alia has built a path that bridges technical education and real-world impact. She doesn't just study the planet's problems — she works on solving them, one project, one policy, one conversation at a time.

Formally selected as one of 200 Climate Envoys by Turkey's Ministry of Environment, Urbanisation and Climate Change — a distinction earned as an international student, making the selection all the more significant — she serves as a bridge between academic communities and national climate action. As a youth delegate at COY19 in Baku and a participant in the GreenX MENA Regional Forum, she has stood in rooms where decisions are made and made her voice heard.

Through Green Gate 4 MENA Youth, YOUNGO's ACE Working Group, and the Anna Lindh Foundation's Euro-Mediterranean programme, Alia contributes actively to climate discourse and youth empowerment. She believes deeply that the next generation doesn't just inherit the climate crisis — they will define its outcome.

02 — Journey

The path so far.

2019 – 2022

The Foundation

Graduated from Ismailia STEM High School — a science-first education that ignited a lifelong curiosity about engineering and the natural world. Completed 3 Capstone projects tackling real environmental challenges facing Egypt, from water scarcity to renewable energy. The beginning of a pattern: learning by solving.

2020 – 2021

First Steps Into Impact

Joined the New York Academy of Sciences' Junior Academy and 1000 Girls 1000 Futures program, and participated in the EducationUSA Academy Connects - Application Ready program by University of Colorado Boulder in the US. Named Best Fellow at Emonovo, logging 590+ working hours.

2022 – 2023

Crossing Borders

Moved to Istanbul. Mastered Turkish through the TÖMER program at Istanbul University-Cerrahpaşa in less than 8 months. A bold leap that proved cultural immersion accelerates growth like nothing else.

2023 – Present

Engineering Meets Advocacy

Enrolled in Environmental Engineering at Istanbul University-Cerrahpaşa — the technical foundation that gives her advocacy real weight. As her academic roots deepened, so did her leadership: from Operations to Chief Operating Officer at Green Gate 4 MENA Youth, and now Head of Programs & Impact. A natural progression from doing the work to shaping its direction.

Nov 2024

The Global Stage

Represented Egypt as Youth Delegate at COY19 — the 19th UN Climate Change Conference of Youth — in Baku, Azerbaijan. One of the loudest rooms in global climate politics for youth, and she had a seat at the table.

2025

Ambassador, Speaker, Engineer

Appointed Climate Envoy by Turkey's Ministry of Environment. Keynote speaker at LCOY Türkiye. Intenal Operations Coordinator at GreenX MENA. Lab intern in wastewater treatment. ALF training in Euro-Mediterranean climate advocacy. A year of convergence.

2026

Erasmus+ Exchange — Hungary

Currently on exchange at Ludovika University of Public Service in Hungary, deepening expertise in Water Science and Environmental Engineering. The story continues.

03 — Impact

Work that matters.

Youth Leadership

Green Gate 4 MENA Youth

Challenge: MENA youth lacked a centralised, accessible gateway to sustainability opportunities — scholarships, conferences, fellowships — and a platform to tackle youth empowerment challenges across the region.

Role: Chief Operating Officer (COO, 2024–2026), now Head of Programs & Impact (2026–present) — led the platform's operations, launched the inaugural Fellowship, built a cross-national team, and continues to shape the organisation's programmatic direction.

Connected hundreds of MENA youth to international sustainability opportunities; currently driving the next phase of youth empowerment programming.
Regional Forum

GreenX MENA Regional Youth Forum

Challenge: Orchestrating a multi-country regional youth forum on climate action with complex cross-border logistics.

Role: Internal Operations & Coordinating Director — led a diverse international team of youth from across the MENA region, coordinated workflow, and ensured seamless delivery of the forum.

Successfully delivered a landmark regional sustainability forum in Tunisia, Oct 2025.
Policy & Advocacy

Climate Envoy Programme
